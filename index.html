WORK PROCESS

To create a high-quality piece it is important to follow this production process in each project.

1. PREPRODUCTION

Phase where our clients give us a brief and indications of everything they want in the piece. At this point, we need all the visual references and specifications necessary to achieve an accurate preliminary sketch with the ideas that are transmitted to us.

2. PRODUCTION

Once the sketch is approved by our clients, the color and rendering phase begins. This is where we achieve the result that our clients need.

3. POST-PRODUCTION

The work is almost finished. In post-production, we add visual effects, small corrections that our clients want, and color corrections. After all this, we have our final piece.
